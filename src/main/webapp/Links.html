Durango Colorado

Durango Is a beautiful mountain town located in southwest Colorado. It began as a mining town supporting the many mines in the area in the 1880's. Since then it has grown into a booming tourist destination, as well as a wonderful place to live.

This location is 0.4 miles from the Lost Dog reception site, roughly 4 blocks. (15 Rooms Reserved)

03

Durango-La Plata County Airport
Flying into Durango is a bit more of a challenge than a major city. Currently only United, US Airways, and American Airlines fly here. Denver, Phoenix, and Dallas/Fort Worth are the only routes in and out. The Airport itself is 15 miles outside of town, info about rental cars and shuttle services can be found at the Airport website.

04

Durango Tourism Info
If you are looking for alternative lodging, places to eat, or just things to do in your down time, the Durango Tourism Office website is the place to go.

05

Durango Transit
Durango is fairly small and most of downtown is within walking distance of the hotels above, but if you want to see more, check out the Durango Transit system.
