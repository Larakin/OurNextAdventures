About Shannon

After growing up and attending College in Oklahoma (State) and relocating several times to New Orleans and Little Rock, Arkansas, I was finally able to make my way to the mountains.

The contrast of the mountains with the desert in the Four Corners area was just what I needed to make a new start. It was Pete, my trusty coonhound, and I against the world. Once settled in Durango, I made some new friends and began to enjoy the new landscape.

About Justin

I grew up in Park City Utah, spending my winters skiing and summers mountain biking and camping. During high school summer breaks, I explored the Black Hills of South Dakota.

Upon graduating from Park City High School, I found Fort Lewis College in Durango. Once here, I knew this was my home. After earning my degree in Computer Science from “The Fort”, I was one of the lucky few to land a job in town. I bought a house from my good friend Jeff and have continued to live happy since.

About Us

It all began with a chance meeting among friends at a local brewery, Steamworks. We were introduced and were instantly captivated. The night ended and we went our separate ways without knowing how to contact one another. A few months and some internet stalking later, we randomly met up again through friends at Ska Brewing. This time we made sure to exchange contact info. Within a few days, we had gone out on our first date.

Since that time, we have spend each winter skiing and snowshoeing and each summer traveling, camping and backpacking. Most Fridays you will find us with friends for happy hour at either Lost Dog or North Home Slice. On any given Sunday we will be bellied up to the bar at Carver’s for breakfast and mimosas.

Justin and I were finally able to go on a backpacking trip this summer (2014) and after the clouds parted on our first day, he proposed. Apparently everyone knew that it was going to happen except me. And of course I said “um yeah”. We celebrated with sips from a whisky flask and a red wine from a bladder.

We most cherish our time scheming where to go and what to do next. Whether it be backpacking in the Weminuche Wilderness, sailing across the Atlantic, or visiting friends and family across the US and Korea, we excitedly look forward to our next great adventures together.
